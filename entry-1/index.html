What's buzzing in the world of Ai

Entry #1 – what is Ai 2027.com

The AI-2027 website presents a scenario about how artificial intelligence might develop over the next few years, focusing on the possibility of rapid and dramatic change. It suggests that by 2027, AI systems could become “superhuman” in certain tasks, especially through automating AI research and development itself. In this vision, each generation of AI agents builds better, cheaper, and more capable successors, creating a feedback loop that accelerates progress. The scenario explores two possible directions: one where competition pushes countries and companies to advance as quickly as possible, even at the expense of safety, and another where development slows because of regulation and careful alignment work.
